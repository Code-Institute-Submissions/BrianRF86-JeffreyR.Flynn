What do I do?

I am an artist who is looking develop a body of work that is environmentally informative. The scuttling crab, the dancing sea horses, the proud lobster and the infinite coastal wildlife that thrive in the roaring waves. These are the things I endeavour to replicate, in transposing their nature into sculpture, I wish to channel my memories and love of the coast, its hidden worlds and its inhabitants. Through this channelling I aim to inspire in others the same contemplation, the same understanding, the same will to preserve and assist. I aim to assure that our futures are not void of these worlds and their life. I want you to hear the booming symphony of the coast while sitting in traffic. I want to immerse you in nature while you stand surrounded by walls.
